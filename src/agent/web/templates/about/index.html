Product tour

From messy facts to live listings — with proof it learns.

Catalogsmith is an AI catalog agent for 1–2 operators. You paste product facts in plain English, review every draft at a human gate, and publish to a real storefront. Over time, the agent needs fewer edits because it remembers your taste.

You teach the agent your taste

  1. Paste raw product facts

What's included

✓
Parse & validate

Structures messy input into a valid product record — price, category, features.

✓
Grounded descriptions

Drafts stay tied to the facts you provided. Grounding checks flag unsupported claims.

✓
Human approval gate

Nothing publishes without your sign-off. Edit, reject with feedback, or approve.

✓
Live storefront

Approved products appear on the shop immediately. Refresh to see updates.

✓
Feedback memory

Chroma stores your voice rules and corrections. Edit-rate graph proves learning.

✓
Guardrails

Input sanitizer strips injection attempts. Grounding violations surface at the gate.
